Strategic Research Period — Gift-Giver Pivot Validated

Nearly Newborn · MMai Platform · Report #2 — April 21 – May 23, 2026

This reporting period was intentionally non-executional. All 9 sequences were paused while the team conducted deep audience research across two angles: expecting-parent-direct and baby-gifter. The expecting-parent audience was audited and found non-viable — after removing age 35+ and failed skiptrace records, only ~57 real contacts with valid ages under 35 remained (~30 female). That's below the viable threshold for a self-enrollment expecting-parent sequence. The decisive finding: baby gifters (grandparents, friends, family) represent the strongest — and now sole — product-market fit. Nearly Newborn is a digital course that can be gifted, and the most natural buyer is someone searching for a meaningful baby shower or newborn gift. The new flagship sequence, GifterCribIntent (623 contacts), targets the "Crib Mattress and Bedding" purchase-intent signal — people actively buying for a baby. 4 steps configured, contacts loaded, ready to deploy the moment the email integration is reconnected. All-time metrics remain at baseline (3,781 executions, 64 opens, 1.69% open rate). The single active blocker is email reconnection. Once resolved, the gift-only angle can be tested, with the expectation that gift-focused messaging will outperform the baseline parent-direct approach on opens, clicks, and course signups.

🎯 Key Strategic Pivot
Shift from "Are you expecting?" to "Know someone expecting? Here's a gift they'll actually use." — targeting gift-givers via purchase-intent signals (Crib Mattress and Bedding searches).

💡Performance Insights

Key observations from the 33-Day Interim period (April 21 – May 23, 2026).

1
Expecting-Parent Audience Non-Viable — Gift-Giver Angle Is the Only Path Forward
The team audited the expecting-parent audience this period and found it below viable threshold: after removing age 35+ contacts and failed skiptrace records, only ~57 real contacts with valid ages remained (~30 female). That's insufficient for a self-enrollment expecting-parent sequence. The Nursery Prep Parent sequence (1,004 raw contacts) was built before this audit and is now deprioritized. The decisive finding: baby gifters are the sole viable audience. The new GifterCribIntent sequence (623 contacts) targets "Crib Mattress and Bedding" purchase-intent signals — people actively buying for a baby — and represents the only path forward.
2
Product-Aligned Audience Strategy: Gifters, Not Parents
Nearly Newborn is a digital course for new and expecting parents that can also be gifted — a key distinction from service-based MMai clients. The most natural buyer is someone looking for a meaningful baby shower or newborn gift. The GifterCribIntent sequence targets people actively searching for "Crib Mattress and Bedding" — strong intent signals that they're buying for a baby, making them ideal gift-course prospects. This shift from parent-direct to gift-focused messaging is expected to improve open rates, click rates, and course signups vs. the baseline.
3
Zero Campaign Activity — By Design, Not Inaction
All 9 sequences remained paused this period. 3,781 total executions, 64 opens (1.69%), and 0 clicks are unchanged from the baseline — confirming no sends occurred. This was intentional: deploying additional volume into the pre-pivot parent-direct messaging would have burned contacts before the gift-giver strategy was ready.
4
Email Integration Disconnected — Single Active Blocker
The sender email needs to be reconnected in MMai before any sequence can execute. This is the only remaining blocker to go-live for the Gift-Giver sequence. All other infrastructure is in place: 4-step sequence built, 623 contacts loaded, schedule configured, CTA aligned to free course signup.
5
No Bookings Expected — Conversion Model Is Course Signup, Not Appointment
Unlike service-based MMai clients, Nearly Newborn's conversion path is: email → click → course landing page → free signup → paid upgrade. Booking metrics are not applicable. Success metrics going forward should focus on click-through rate to the course signup page and free-to-paid conversion tracking once that integration is built.
6
623 Gift-Intent Contacts + 1,117 Baby Prep Keywords — 1,740 Deployable
The GifterCribIntent sequence has 623 contacts loaded targeting the "Crib Mattress and Bedding" purchase-intent signal — the highest-quality audience layer. The Baby Prep Keywords list (1,117 contacts) — flagged in Report #1 as the largest untapped pool — can be redirected toward the gift angle. Together, these represent 1,740 contacts ready for gift-focused engagement once the email blocker is resolved. The Nursery Prep Parent sequence (1,004 contacts) is deprioritized after the expecting-parent audience audit.
