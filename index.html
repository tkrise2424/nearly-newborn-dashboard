Baseline Intelligence Summary

Nearly Newborn · MMai Platform · October 2025 – April 20, 2026

Nearly Newborn has built a meaningful outreach infrastructure over the past six months — deploying 7 sequences, enrolling 2,524 contacts, and executing 3,781 outreach touches across Email and SMS. The AI Audience Builder has identified 2,310 intent-qualified contacts across 5 lists, with the most precisely targeted layer being the AI Intent Data: Baby Gifts list (460 contacts, "Crib Mattress and Bedding" purchase-intent signal). Email deliverability is confirmed with 64 unique opens at a 1.69% open rate — consistent with cold outreach norms at this stage. The critical gap is below the open: zero clicks and zero replies across all 3,781 executions indicate that while the audience is receiving messages, the content is not yet driving action. SMS is generating inbound responses but they are predominantly opt-outs and wrong-number contacts, pointing to list phone data quality as a friction point. The most immediate opportunity is the 333 pending approvals sitting in queue — activating these with refined, benefit-driven messaging could meaningfully shift engagement in the next period.

💡Performance Insights

Data-backed observations from the baseline period. Pattern-based — not generic.

1
Email deliverability is healthy — the problem is below the open
A 1.69% open rate on cold outreach is within normal range and confirms that emails are reaching inboxes. The bounce rate of 0.11% is excellent. The issue is not deliverability — it is message relevance and CTA strength. Contacts are opening and immediately disengaging, producing zero clicks and zero replies across 3,781 touches.
2
SMS is generating noise, not leads — phone list quality needs review
The majority of AI agent SMS conversations are opt-outs ("STOP"), wrong numbers, and contacts who explicitly state they are not expecting. This pattern suggests that the phone numbers appended to the intent audience lists contain a high proportion of mismatched or stale data. Scaling SMS volume without fixing this will increase opt-out rates and risk carrier flagging.
3
The "Crib Mattress and Bedding" intent signal is the strongest audience layer deployed
The AI Intent Data – Baby Gifts list (460 contacts) is the most precisely targeted audience in the account, built from active purchase-intent signals rather than demographic inference alone. This list powers the only currently active sequence and should be the primary focus for messaging refinement and CTA testing in the next period.
4
333 pending approvals represent the fastest path to increased output
These executions are built and queued but have not been sent. Approving them with current messaging would add immediate volume, but the higher-value move is to revise the CTA before releasing them. A benefit-specific, urgency-anchored CTA tied to the free tier ("Get the free Nearly Newborn guide — no commitment") is likely to outperform the current link-drop approach.
5
The Gift Giver audience segment has not been fully activated
Two Gift Giver sequences (825 combined members) are inactive. This audience — relatives and friends of expecting parents — is a distinct buyer persona that likely responds to different messaging than the expecting parent themselves. Reactivating this segment with gift-focused copy ("Know someone expecting? Here's a gift they'll actually use") could open a second engagement channel with minimal additional list-building effort.
